A Trip to Nicaragua

Have you ever wanted to go to Nicaragua?

This nation was probably not on the top of your mind, especially since Central America has other countries to offer, like Costa Rica or Mexico. Nicaragua offers mindblowing views, great hikes, and even volcano boarding! Today, I will be showing you my complete 4-day vacation plan to Nicaragua, with calculations and the like. Oh, and you will be able to calculate yours too. Bring your notebook and pencil for the ride!

Notes

  • It's worth mentioning that this travel guide was made with a friend coming along. I'll name him John Doe.
  • I will be using Canadian Dollars in this travel report.
  • The vacation at Nicaragua is 4 days long, BUT the total length of the trip is 8 days. 4 of the days is spent on flying, and waiting for the next plane ride to arrive.
  • Please check your national travel advisories before making this trip
